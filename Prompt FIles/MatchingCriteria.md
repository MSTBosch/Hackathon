##Criteria of Match Strucutre:   
- A concise summary of project in text 
- Calendar year of delivery 
- timeline of project delivery duration, from first purchase to signoff of SAT as a duration in months 
- sell price of project in AUD 
- location of customer site and the installation of the project hardware 
- lessons learned document present or not, as well as a hyperlink to it's location 
- Explain why the match was made and how it is relevant 
- A Confidence score based off the information which has been found 
- Industry or customer sector
o	Product or system type
o	Manufacturing process (e.g. assembly, testing, welding, inspection)
o	Automation level (manual, semi-automated, fully automated)
o	Technology used (e.g. robotics, vision, PLC, fixtures, test systems)
o	Commercial scope (consulting, concept, RFQ, design, build, commissioning)
o	Scale and complexity (prototype, pilot, high-volume, multi-line)
o	Risk profile (e.g. tolerancing, immature design, supplier variability)
